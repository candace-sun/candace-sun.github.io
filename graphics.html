GRAPHIC DESIGN

I love the design process, and this page is brief glimpse into what I've come up with!

I really first got interested in graphic design when I discovered Canva in middle school, and it was such a fun way to elevate my school projects. Since then, I've been chasing design in my free time - I've experimented with tools such as the Adobe suite, GIMP and my favorite online editor, MiniPaint, while working as a Yearbook editor and magazine designer in high school.

More recently, I've served as a graphic designer for the Chinese Student Association at UMD, and have supported marketing efforts of various other organizations.
